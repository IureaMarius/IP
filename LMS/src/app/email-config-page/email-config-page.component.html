In order to use the email, you will need to: 1. Enable two factor authenticator on your personal Google account 2. Manually generate a password from your Google account in order to be able to use the mailing service
  • Open the Security tab
  • Under "Signing in to Google", click on App passwords
  • In the Select App dropdown, pick Mail
  • In the Select Device dropdown, pick Windows Computer
  • Click on Generate and copy the 16-letter password
  • Enter the password in our configuration page
